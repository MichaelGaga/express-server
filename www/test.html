Colloseesze.

Colloseesze. Also know as Godson, King Kupake. A 23 year old Zimbabwean Hip Hop artist based on the Gold Coast (QLD) Australia. With a huge amount of his work based on rhyme and poetry (Rap). He is also a producer, having started out strictly making beats until recently. Making a complete switch towards vocals and production.

Colloseesze Has A broad skillset comprising of:

  • Song Writting.
  • Audio Engineering.
  • Live perfoming.
  • Production.

Colloseesze is know for his collaborative work with the Collective Vibe Collective in Gold Coast Australia. Having produced over 20 songs with the label. Colloseesze also has his own label called Lxne Records. But its more of a solo label at the moment focused on him.
